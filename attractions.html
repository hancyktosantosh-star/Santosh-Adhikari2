Top Attractions of Nepal

Nepal is famous for its beautiful mountains, ancient temples, and natural landscapes. From the world’s highest peak Mount Everest to peaceful places like Lumbini and Pokhara etc, Nepal offers many amazing attractions. These places reflect the country’s natural beauty, culture, and history.

Mount Everest

The world's tallest mountain and a must-see destination for trekkers and climbers from around the globe.



Mount Everest

Pashupatinath Temple

A sacred Hindu temple on the banks of the Bagmati River, famous for its architecture and spiritual significance.



Pashupatinath Temple

Boudhanath Stupa

The largest stupas in the world and a UNESCO World Heritage Site, representing Nepal’s Buddhist heritage.



Boudhanath Stupa

Lumbini

The birthplace of Lord Buddha, attracting tourists seeking peace and spirituality.


Lumbini

Pokhara

Pokhara, the city of lakes, is the second-largest city of Nepal after Kathmandu.


Pokhara
