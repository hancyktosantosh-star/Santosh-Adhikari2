Nepal

A land of mountains, culture, and ancient traditions.

Introduction

Nepal is a beautiful country located in South Asia, known for its landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. It is home to the world's highest mountain and diverse traditions.




Geography & Nature

Nepal is divided into three main regions: the Himalayan mountains, the hilly region, and the Terai plains. The country is famous for snow-capped peaks, rivers, forests, and national parks.




Culture & People

Nepal is home to more than 125 ethnic groups living together peacefully. Each community has its own language, traditions, festivals, and traditional dress, making Nepal culturally rich and diverse.




Religion & Heritage

Nepal is the birthplace of Lord Buddha and a sacred land for both Hinduism and Buddhism. The country has many ancient temples, stupas, and UNESCO World Heritage Sites.




Why Visit Nepal?

  • Adventure activities like trekking and hiking
  • Beautiful mountains and natural scenery
  • Rich culture and colorful festivals
  • Friendly and welcoming people


Popular Tourist Activities

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Kathmandu
  • Language: Nepali
  • Currency: Nepalese Rupee
  • Highest Peak: Mount Everest
